About Us

Understanding the fire hazzard through its chemistry is crucial.

From engineering point of view, fire is a chemical chain reaction. It is important to estimate the fire load of a building and understand the occupants beheaviors inside the building during normal operation. Applying the fire and building code, an appropriate protection, such as Fire Alarm System for automatic fire/smoke detection and Autometic Sprinkler System for suppressing the fire to be designed and installed.

Ziaul Islam, who leads the design team, is a strong one, with years of experience in fire protection designing field. The team members works on virtual mode and serving different customers in different countries. Good investments has been made on the design department. Tri-zone has invested on high end software that the developing company has 50 years of experience in the fire protection industry including fire sprinklers and water mist, ranging from million pound complex industrial sprinkler systems to domestic and residential protection. The software also does the hydraulic calculations for sprinkler and standpipe system.

The designing team has been trained to work on the software and producing perfect designs and hydraulic calculations according to NFPA 13 and 14 standards.
