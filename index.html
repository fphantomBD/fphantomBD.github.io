WHY CHOOSE US ?

Quality

Our specialization enables us to ensure the highest standards and to fully satisfy all expectations and requirements made by our customers. We follow a quality plan of international standards. Quality Assurance is conducted in 4 stages of production. 1.Pre Production Check (PPC), 2.Initial Production Check( IPC), 3.During Productions Check (DUPRO), 4.Final Random Inspection (FRI)

Our Vision

To be one of the leading exporter of garments in Bangladesh maintaining a standardized socially responsible quality management system to ensure higher levels of (a) personal service, (b) quality consistency, (c) delivery lead times, (d) product innovation, (e) price competitiveness, (f) reliability, and (g) compliance with corporate codes of conduct.

Our Mission

Strive to satisfy and to fulfill customers' expectations and to be accurate and on time with all deliveries.
